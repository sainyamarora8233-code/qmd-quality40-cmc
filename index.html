"We've seen this before. Another hype cycle."

The fatigue is earned. But AI is not a methodology — it's infrastructure. It's already embedded in the ERP systems, inspection cameras, and supplier networks quality professionals manage every day. The question isn't whether to adopt it. It's whether quality practitioners lead that adoption or get bypassed by it.

The CMC Initiative isn't here to sell you on AI. It's here to make sure quality professionals — not vendors — define how it integrates with ISO-based QMS. Skeptics are exactly who should be in the room.
