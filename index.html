ISO 42001 — The Bridge Standard You May Not Know

Published December 2023, ISO 42001 is the first international standard for AI Management Systems — and it's designed to integrate with ISO 9001, ISO 13485, and AS9100. If your organization is anywhere on this slider, this standard applies to you. One of Phase 1's first deliverables is a plain-language mapping of ISO 42001 to common QMS clause structures.
